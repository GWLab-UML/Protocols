# GoPro Deployment
Protocol for assembly and deployment

## Stand Assembly
#### Materials:
for one stand:
- X# X" PVC (for the base)
- 1 X" PVC (for the vertical stand)
- 1 5-way PVC connector
- 4 ¾" slip cap
- PVC cement
- Grout
- Power drill
- Thin nylon rope (little less than 1m) for GoPro slip knots
- Thicker line (~3.5m) for buoy attachment
- 1 Buoy (water bottle works just fine!)
- Zipties
- 1 Rubberband

#### Assembly:
1. Cut PVC for the base legs - add slip caps to the ends
2. Mix grout until a thick liquid consistency - pipe into the PVC 
3. Store upright until the grout has hardened
4. Attach the 4 legs to the 5-way PVC connector with PVC cement 
5. Cut PVC for the vertical stand - drill 3 holes
	- 1" from bottom with X drill bit
	- 1" from top with X drill bit
	- middle of PVC with X drill bit
6. Attach the vertical stand to the 5-way connector with PVC cement
7. Feed the thin nylon rope through the middle hole on the vertical stand - tie a slip knot on either end (this will go around the GoPros to catch them if they fall down during deployment)
8. Feed the thicker rope in the top hole and tie off
9. Ziptie a water bottle or gallon jug to the end of the thicker rope
10. Ziptie a rubber band to the thick rope at the top - the rubberband can be fed through the middle of the coiled rope and looped around the top of the stand to keep the line contained and organized
