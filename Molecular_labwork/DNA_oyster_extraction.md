## Notes
Qiagen Blood & Tissue DNA Extraction Protocol
Used for PIC_Aug_2025 extractions (shotgun metagenomics)

## Materials
- Bunsen burner
- 2x forceps
- 2x dissecting scissors
- 100% ethanol
- Face mask
- 1.5mL tubes (1 per sample)
- 1.5mL DNA lo-bind tubes (1 per sample)
- Kit reagents
	- ATL buffer
	- Proteinase K
	- AL buffer
	- AW1 buffer
	- AW2 buffer
- *Sterile* 100% ethanol (200uL per sample)
- EB buffer aliquot (150uL per sample)

## 0. Prep
- Set heating block to 56ºC and start warming EB buffer aliquot(s)
- Add ethanol to buffers AW1 and AW2 (only if opening a new bottle)
- UV sterilize 1.5mL tubes 
- Set up bunsen burner, forceps, dissecting scissors, and ethanol and spray bench with bleach

## 1. Prepare samples
**Perform the following steps on the bench with a lit bunsen burner to ensure a sterile field. Wear a mask to prevent contamination.**

1. Using a set of sterile forceps and dissecting scissors, carefully cut the tissue sample roughly in half
2. Transfer one piece of tissue into a sterile 1.5mL tube
3. Sterilize (ethanol-flame) forceps and dissecting scissors between samples
4. After all samples have been moved, return original tubes with the remaining sample to the freezer
	- *Make sure the tissue is completely submerged in the RNAlater!* 

## 2. Tissue lysis 
1. In the BSC, add 180uL ATL buffer to and 20uL proteinase K to each sample and vortex
2. Incubate at 56ºC until completely lysed, ~30 min
	- Vortex occasionally during this incubation
3. Once sample is completely lysed vortex for 15s immediately before proceeding
4. Add 200uL AL buffer and vortex
5. Incubate at 56ºC for ten minutes

## 3. Wash and Elute
1. Add 200uL ethanol to each sample and vortex
2. Transfer the samples (600uL) into labeled spin columns and centrifuge at 6,000g for 1 minute—discard the filtrate and collection tube
3. Using new collection tubes, add 500uL AW1 buffer to each spin column and centrifuge at 6,000g for 1 minute—discard the filtrate and collection tube
4. Using new collection tubes again, add 500uL AW2 buffer to each spin column and centrifuge at 20,000g for 3 minute—discard the filtrate and collection tube
5. Transfer the column into a 1.5mL DNA lo-bind tube
	- *This is where the DNA will be stored long-term, so label fully*
6. Add 150uL warm EB buffer onto the center of the column filter and incubate for 1 minute
7. Centrifuge at 6,000g for 1 minute
8. Proceed to DNA quantification and then store at -20ºC
	- *Vortex briefly before quantification*
