**ImageJ general tips**
+ + / - keys to zoom in and out
- Hold space bar to move photo
- Set scale before taking measurements
- Use polygon tool (much easier with a mouse)
- Press ctrl/cmd-m to measure selected area
- Sum boring area per shell before recording in csv
